About

A critical thinker, creative designer and visual communicator

I'm a final year undergraduate at National University of Singapore, majoring in Computational Biology and Life Sciences (GPA: 4.93/5.00). Computational Biology, the most multi-disciplinary and allegedly-hardest science major, assisted me in cultivating technical capabilities in data visualization, UI/UX design and database management.

I’ve always had an intense passion for art and design. Ever since I can recall, I have enjoyed practising various kinds of paintings such as comics, watercolor painting, figure drawing, traditional Chinese painting and oil painting. In addition, I have concentrated on learning design tools such as HTML, CSS, Adobe AI and employed them to design posters.

Design is a harmonious combination of my joy of problem solving with my enthusiasm for visual appeal. I am always striving for effective design with high anesthetic values. Particularly, my strengths for user experience design lie in bioinformatic interface design and game scenery design. I’m always open to new design challenges and eager to share inspiration with others. Please feel free to get in touch regarding collaboration or general discussion.
